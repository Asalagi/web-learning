My Favorite Book Series

By John Gwynne

The Faithful and The Fallen

This is a 4 book series. And is absolutely my favorite! Malice is the first book. Valor the second. Ruin the third, which will literally ruin you! And Wrath the fourth.

of Blood and Bone

This is a series about the desecendants of the characters from The Faithful and The Fallen series. It is base 120 years after the events of The FaithFul and the Fallen. It is a triology, the first book a Time of Blood, second is a Time of Dread, and third a Time of Courage.

The Bloodsworn

This series is based of norse mythology. Exploring the views of three main characters, Orka a mother trying to live a quiet life with her familt. Varg seeking closure of a loved one he lost. And Elvar a young warrior wanting to gain her battle fame. This series is made up of two books, Shadow of the Gods and Hungry of the Gods. The third book is still in the making.
